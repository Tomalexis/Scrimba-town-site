Visit Neuilly

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Paris.

Top three activities to do in Neuilly

Attend a show or a concert

The Neuilly theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a picknic in the local park

The Saint-James Park is perfect for picknics, getting some tan or just chilling out.

Swim at the municipal pool

The pool includes a jacuzzi, a slide and sport equipments for everyone.
